About Team Ryu

Team Ryu is a leading team in the field of miniature car engineering, dedicated to designing and building high-performance miniature cars. We are passionate about pushing the boundaries of miniaturized automotive technology and delivering exceptional results in every competition.

Our team thrives on collaboration and innovation, focusing on every detail to ensure our cars are not only functional but also competitive. With a commitment to excellence and a drive to inspire future engineers, we continuously refine our designs and techniques to stay ahead in the world of miniature car racing.

Sustainability Initiatives

Team Ryu is committed to sustainability and has implemented several initiatives to promote environmental responsibility. We have conducted assemblies at our school to reduce paper waste and encourage the use of metal cups instead of disposable paper cups.

Our BYOC (Bring Your Own Cup) initiative encourages students and staff to bring metal cups from home, reducing reliance on single-use paper cups provided by the school. This effort is part of our broader commitment to minimizing environmental impact and fostering a culture of sustainability.
